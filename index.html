Chicken Stew

This chicken is a delicacy all across Kenya. Here's how to prepare it:

Ingredients

  • Kienyeji chicken
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

Procedure

  1. Boil the chicken with salt, garlic, and ginger until tender.
  2. In a pan, heat cooking oil until hot.
  3. Add your boiled chicken in batches. Fry until brown.
  4. In a separate sufuria (pot), fry the onion until it starts browning.
  5. Add the fried chicken and stir.
  6. Add blended tomatoes and cover the sufuria.
  7. Cook on low heat until the tomatoes are fully cooked.
  8. Add a little water and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with dhania (coriander).
